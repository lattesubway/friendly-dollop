Pacific Palisades doesn't have many small, owner-operated grooming studios — most options are either chain shops further inland or mobile services that can be hard to schedule. The Pet Affaire is fifteen minutes south on PCH or Sunset, with a quiet street-level studio and same-day text updates so you're never wondering how things are going.

We see a lot of Palisades coats: long-haired retrievers and doodles that need real scissor work, anxious rescues that need a slow first visit, and cats that need a dog-free morning to stay calm. We schedule with all of that in mind.
