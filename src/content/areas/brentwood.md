Brentwood has a lot of well-bred dogs and a lot of long-haired cats — both of which deserve a groomer who can do real scissor work and won't rush the visit. We've built a long client list out of Brentwood Glen, Crestwood Hills, and the streets south of Sunset, mostly through word of mouth from neighbors.

We book one pet at a time and always confirm pricing before we begin, so a Brentwood standard or a Persian who hasn't been groomed in a while never ends up with a surprise on the receipt.
