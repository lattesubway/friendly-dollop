Venice is full of dog people, and full of grooming options that have drifted toward assembly-line work at boutique prices. The Pet Affaire is the other end of that spectrum: small, owner-operated, scissor finishing on every cut, and one pet at a time so nobody waits in a kennel all day.

If you live anywhere in 90291, you can be at our door in under ten minutes. Cat clients especially appreciate that we book dog-free windows so the studio stays quiet — most cats settle in immediately when there's no barking next door.
