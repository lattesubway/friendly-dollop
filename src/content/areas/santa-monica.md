The Pet Affaire has been part of the Santa Monica neighborhood for a long time. Generations of clients — from Sunset Park families with first puppies to North-of-Montana cats who've been coming since they were kittens — keep finding their way back, and we keep doing the work the same way: one pet at a time, no all-day waits, no rushed hands.

Most of our clients live within a five-minute drive. We're on Lincoln Boulevard between Pico and Ocean Park, with street parking on Lincoln and the side streets, and easy access from anywhere in the 90402, 90403, 90404, or 90405 ZIPs.

If you're new to the neighborhood and looking for a Santa Monica groomer who'll learn your dog's coat or your cat's tolerance — and remember it next time — we'd love to meet you both.
